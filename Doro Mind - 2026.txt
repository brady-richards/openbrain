Starting Point—A Family in Crisis
Last autumn, Marisol’s symptoms of psychosis and debilitating anxiety had intensified. She felt constantly nauseous from her medications, struggled to get out of bed, and withdrew entirely from her once-beloved community art studio. Her mother, Inez, worried for her daughter’s safety and future, unsure how to help.

Our Integrated Approach—Beyond Medication Alone:
Our team assembled and coordinated a care plan, blending careful medication titration, the filling of gaps in care delivery, side-effect management, and targeted therapy sessions. We helped find a number of new care providers, introduced a new nighttime Clozapine schedule to improve her sleep, and adjusted her regimen to relieve persistent GI issues — ensuring she felt physically stable enough to focus on recovery.

Empowering Family—Building a Supportive Network:
Inez joined our family guidance sessions, learning how to communicate openly without triggering Marisol’s anxieties. She discovered how to set boundaries, encourage Marisol’s strengths, and engage calmly when setbacks occurred. As trust grew, the home became a safer, more hopeful environment. Inez also found new confidence in navigating the health system and leaned on the care team to help accomplish tasks. 
Breakthrough Moments—Reclaiming Her Life
Within weeks, Marisol’s pacing and agitation lessened. Soon, she felt well enough to attend a small, three-hour community art event—her first public outing in months. She reconnected with old acquaintances, shared a heartfelt conversation with her art instructor, and rediscovered the simple joy of creating something beautiful.
Lasting Change—A Path Forward
Today, Marisol reports more stable moods, stronger relationships, and renewed motivation to pursue her interests. Her mother, Inez, now feels equipped, not just anxious. Together, they’ve moved from crisis to alliance, showcasing the profound impact of a truly integrated care model. Their path to recovery is well underway and Doro Mind will be there for them every step of the way.
